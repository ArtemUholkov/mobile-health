How it Works?

1

Register Your Account

Click the provided link to create your personal account. Registration is quick and easy - just enter your basic information to get started.

2

Choose your therapy

Once your medical information is filled, you will purchase the weight loss therapy of your choice. You can complete your order online with confidence, knowing your treatment is medically supported.

3

Complete the Medical Intake Form

After registration, you'll be asked to fill out a detailed medical intake form. This form helps our medical team understand your health background and ensure the therapy is safe and tailored specifically for you.

4

Receive your medications

After purchase, your medication will be shipped discreetly to your home. Follow the usage instructions provided by our healthcare professionals to start your weight loss journey safely and effectively. Our team remains available to support you every step of the way.
